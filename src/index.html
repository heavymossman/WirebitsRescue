About Bywire

ByWire is a disruptive organisation that is revolutionising the news industry by utilising the power of the blockchain, cryptocurrencies and smart contracts.

Building upon lessons learned from political activism, publishing, journalism and psephology, our team understands from experience the need for urgent and permanent change.

We feel our vision of independence, attributability and decentralisation is of the moment.

We want to represent readers, publishers and creators alike, broker content, and return profitability, while destroying corruption and removing conflicts from the ways in which our news and media are funded.

ByWire.news will focus on delivering a better connected, truthful world, free from the control of corporate paymasters, vested interests, corrupt governments, state-supported violence and tyranny.

What are Wirebits?

Wirebits will act as the main payment token on both the Ethereum and EOS blockchains, which shall facilitate: both the private and public ICO launches, microtipping, investigative campaign donations, rewards and remuneration.

Creating our ecosystem on EOS, we can innovate a new way of funding the news; at its heart a decentralised and autonomous self governing community.

With the use of microtipping, we can encourage our consumers to ‘tip’ their favourite news content (articles, creators, investigative campaigns, podcasts or videos), providing a consistent, ad-free route to revenue. One that is controlled by the reader.

We already know that quality, in depth content is more likely to be tipped, so let's build a market economy that rejects clickbait, confirmation bias silos and fake news.

ByWire readers can be paid by publishers and ByWire.news to consume content, paid to engage with content and paid to share and promote content that they believe-in. Readers will in-turn reward both the publishers and authors of said work with tips.

This would mean absolute data privacy for our consumers. If we are not tracking you to monitiese you, then we only need to know what you want us to know.
